Instructions for Xavier's Map Mania!

Welcome to Map Mania! The game of Map Mania is simple, you must find locations placed around Europe.
These locations are the places I visited as a backpacking trip with two of my best friends.
On the top of the page you can see both the hints and the score you have.
There is a total of 16 places to found around Europe. There is a maximum of 80 points.

You must be in zoom level 10 to find these places.
Before you start the game you should inspect element on the top white space area and go to console
You will be told in the console if you're inbound and what zoom level you are in.

Scoring Guidelines:
Location Found: +5 points
Hints Used: -2 points

There is a cheat button if you want to be a lazy bum and not complete this game.
