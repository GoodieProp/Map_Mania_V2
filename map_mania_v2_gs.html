Instructions for Xavier's Map Mania!

Welcome to Map Mania! The game of Map Mania is simple, you must find locations placed around Europe. These locations are the places I visited as a backpacking trip with two of my best friends.
If you are stuck, and cannot find the current location, click on the Hint button and you will be given a hint for that location. There will only be 2 hints per location.
You must have your zoom level at 5 or 6 to be able to find the locations. Your scoring will be based off how many locations are found and how many hints used.
There will be two additional buttons, the first one is to quit which will stop the game and give score for the game. The second will be a cheat button, incase you are lazy and a bum.

Scoring Guidelines:
Location Found: +5 points
Hints Used: -2 points

There is a cheat button if you want to be a lazy bum and not complete this game.
